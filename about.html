Various Stake Holders

Fontys Hogeschool

This the Educational Innovator. They represent the educational sector that will adopt and benefit from the immersive tools. Their involvement ensures the tools are practical, user-friendly, and aligned with educational objectives to enhance interactive and engaging learning experiences.

Yonder/Mindlab

MindLabs/Yonder: This is the project primary stakeholder and facilitator. They focus on developing advanced technology driven solutions. As the project owner, it provides resources, expertise, and a collaborative environment where immersive technologies (VR/AR and AI) can be explored, tested, and implemented in educational settings.

Tilburg University

This is the research and ethics experts. The University contributes academic research expertise, particularly in understanding ethical considerations and evaluating the societal impact of implementing VR/AR and AI in education. Their role ensures that the tools developed are inclusive, ethical, and meet diverse educational needs.
